MORNING ROUTINE

https://www.thespruce.com/maintain-window-in-shower-privacy-5213764

Good morning! Welcome to my morning routine!

Compared to the routine explained in the 'Routine Order' page, my routine doesn't include all of those steps. I've mentioned that not all the steps have to be followed, but that the order of the products you use in your routine is very important.


Step 1. Cleanser

Morning cleansing always feels like a wake-up call to me, whether I'm splashing my face with Arctic-level cold water or opening my pores with steamy warm water (depending on the weather that day and how I'm feeling). I prefer to use a water-based cleanser that I first discovered in the Philippines when I was on vacation there in the summer of '22:


Skin by BYS Extra Gentle Jelly Milk Cleanser

Skin by BYS Extra Gentle Jelly Milk Cleanser

This product isn't as well-known as its other Asian counterparts, but I have a feeling it could live up to the Asian skincare hype. It is a South Korean product with very simple, non-irritating ingredients, such as their Botaniceutical Plus 10. These ingredients are harmless to the skin and are found in nature, such as plant extracts and water. It also contains Milk Protein Extract, which hydrates the skin and is very gentle.


Step 2. Essence

I like to skip the toner step in the morning, not because I don't think it's important, but because I am currently a budgeting college student. If I had the money, I would get the Etude House Soon Jung PH 5.5 Relief Toner. However, instead of toner, I go straight to an essence that I consider my holy grail product in my entire routine:


Cosrx Advanced Snail 96 Mucin Power Essence

Cosrx Advanced Snail 96 Mucin Power Essence

Now, I know what you're thinking: Snail Mucin?! And the answer is yes: Snail Mucin! It is a very moisturising ingredient that can also help fight aging or heal wounds like acne scars. The reason why I claim this product to be my holy grail is because no matter how dry my skin is, it manages to restore all moisture back into it. I am a big fan of COSRX and their Snail Mucin line so don't be startled if you see another similar product by them appear later on!


Step 3. Serum

As mentioned before, serums can help target specific skin concerns. Personally, I have been trying to tackle acne scars and hyperpigmentation for many years now and haven't quite found the right product just yet. But this recently new entry might serve to be a contender:


Beauty Joseon Glow Serum: Propolis + Niacinamide

Beauty Joseon Glow Serum: Propolis + Niacinamide

It may sound like a witch's spell but Propolis and Niacinamide are actually very powerful and beneficial ingredients. I have stood by Niacinamide for a long time as it is commonly known to brighten hyperpigmentation but I normally use it to reduce sebum production which is a fancy term for making your skin less oily. Propolis on the other hand is a fairly new agent to my arsenal. Just like Snail Mucin, this ingredient comes from another insect: bees! It fights acne and also brightens skin tone. Overall, this serum leaves my skin glowing and I can already see my acne scars fading slowly.


Step 4. Face Cream

Since I use a lot of products that target different concerns in the morning, I tend to use a moisturiser that is fairly basic as to lock in all the layers of skincare I have already applied.


The Ordinary Natural Moisturizing Factors + HA

The Ordinary Natural Moisturizing Factors + HA

This moisturiser has been very prominent in the skincare industry ever since the first Covid lockdown back in March 2020. Just like many products in this routine, it became popular due to TikTok. The Ordinary brand itself is very reliable for its simple formulas and this moisturiser is no exception. It uses a mix of hydrating ingredients including hyaluronic acid which is widely known to combat dry skin.


Step 5. Sun Cream

Finally, the crema de la cr√®me of skincare products: sun cream. I will admit that I didn't take sun cream as seriously as I should have when I was younger but after lots of research (and TikTok videos of experts begging people to use it) it is a product that I will never forget in my routine. I am very picky with my sun creams and choose them based on two properties: white cast and thickness. Fortunately, I have recently found the best one for me:


Beauty of Joseon Rice Probiotics Sunscreen SPF 50+

Beauty of Joseon Rice Probiotics Sunscreen SPF 50+

Another product from Beauty of Joseon that is worth mentioning is their sun cream. If you're wondering why many Asians have such young and clear skin, it's because we take our sun protection very seriously. This sun cream is lightweight and doesn't cause too much of a white cast, which is a phenomenon where the sun cream may make your skin appear paler. It comes in a 50ml tube, making it perfect for on-the-go.
