About april

april is a blog-style website that I created solely due to my 'healthy-obsession' and long journey with skincare and how I love to try to inspire others to be as passionate about their skin as I am.

It was inspired by my friends, who would beg me to put all my knowledge on the topic somewhere online where they could reread it over and over again instead of struggling to remember it on the spot.

However, I would like to make it clear that I am no skincare expert, a dermatologist or anyone with clearance to advise people on what to do with their skin. I would encourage you to do your own research and consult with a dermatologist or other qualified professional before trying any new skin care products or routines.

Why Skincare?

Like most young teens (except the lucky minority who will always have clear skin no matter what they do) I struggled a lot with acne and other skin concerns. They first appeared when I was in primary school which was not quite the blessing an 11-year-old child would want. In fact, a lot of my confidence was lost during those years because of the severity of my pimples and other skin impurities.

It wasn't until I visited a dermatologist for the first and only time that I learned about the causes of acne and was prescribed an ointment along with skincare tips. Prior to that, I only used a bar of soap and water. Within a day of following the new routine, I noticed significant improvement and was amazed at how simply knowing my skin type and using the right products could make such a difference.

Since then, I have made it my mission to put extra effort in taking care of my skin, learning what ingredients work best for me and building the most efficient morning and night routines. And hopefully this website inspires you to do the same!

Why 'april'?

The name april comes from my own name!

Since the website is mostly about my own interest and my routines and what products I use, then it would only be logical to name my website after me.

(And no, I was not born in the month of April).

How Do You Choose Which Products to Use?

Trial and error.

I have been experimenting with all types of products and ingredients whether it was commercial skincare products or even natural ingredients like honey and tumeric (which I've personally learned to not try again) for as long as eight years and despite having a very solid arsenal of products at the moment, my skin will always be changing.

Because of this, I choose products that suit me in the moment. If my skin is very oily I go for foaming cleansers and non-oily creams. If my skin is dry, I go for hydrating toners and thick moisturisers.

To find the best products for your skin, it's important to be adventurous and try out different options. However, it's crucial to do your research on the ingredients to ensure they are safe and suitable for your skin type.

Do You Have Any Advice on Beginning Skincare?

Patience is a virtue.

Although it has been 8 long years, I still haven't reached my goal of 'glass skin'. I still struggle with hyperpigmentation (also known as 'acne scars') but I am much more confident than I was when I first started. I will forever remain patient in this journey and continue to experiment with various products until I find what truly works for me.

(Don't wait, start your skincare journey now!)
